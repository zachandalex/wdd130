Site Name

Rafting America

Site Purpose with Goals

The purpose of this page is to offer guided adventures for people of all ages. It will also provide opportunities for people to receive training in order to go out on their own safely to explore rafting. The site will also serve as an information portal with information on equipment options and comparisons as well as a list of rafting locations and the difficulty levels of each. The site will mainly be to generate revenue for the Rafting America company through tour bookings and training sessions. The secondary purpose is to help users find information in order to get started rafting.

Target Audience - include at least four scenario descriptions

The target audience for this page will be those who are seeking high adventure. They will enjoy the outdoors and participating in physical activities. They will mostly be young to middle aged and physically fit. They will be financially stable and willing to pay for a guide to take them on their adventure. They want the ease of having the whole trip planned for them so they can just show up and enjoy it. Users will mostly access the site using either a laptop or mobile device. They will be people who are regularly on the go.
